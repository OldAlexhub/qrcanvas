QRCanvas is an offline QR design studio for creating attractive QR codes directly on your Android device.

Create QR codes for websites, plain text, contact cards, Wi-Fi access, emails, phone numbers, SMS messages, calendar events, locations, social/profile links, and custom payloads. Smart forms help you enter the right information for each QR type, while built-in design templates make the final card feel polished and ready to share.

Save your QR projects locally, edit them later, duplicate designs, search your library, and export clean PNG images through the local Android share flow. You can also export JSON backups for individual projects or your local library.

QRCanvas is built for people who want useful QR designs without a website, account, cloud service, ads, or subscriptions.

Highlights:
- Offline QR creation
- Smart forms for common QR types
- Modern design templates

- PNG export
- JSON backup export and import
- No account
