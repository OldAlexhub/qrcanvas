# Feature Graphic Notes

Recommended concept:

Use the QRCanvas logo, a large polished QR card mockup, and the headline "Design beautiful QR codes offline." Keep the visual clean, premium, and utility-focused.

Recommended composition:

- Left or center: QRCanvas app name and tagline.
- Main visual: A modern QR card with title, QR code, and footer note.
- Supporting visual: Small template swatches or cards showing variety.
- Background: Light neutral surface with strong contrast.

Avoid:

- Claims about scan tracking, analytics, cloud sync, or guaranteed results.
- Busy QR patterns that look hard to scan.
- Dark blurred stock imagery.
- Visuals that imply camera scanning or location tracking.
